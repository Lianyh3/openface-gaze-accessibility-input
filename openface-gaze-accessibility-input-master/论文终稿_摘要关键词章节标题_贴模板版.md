【中文题目】
基于OpenFace的实时视线估计优化

【中文摘要】
摘要 面向无障碍交互场景，针对普通摄像头眼控输入在实时应用中存在的注视抖动、空间偏移和误触发问题，本文围绕“可解释、可复现、可部署”的目标开展研究。首先，分析了OpenFace视线观测在真实环境下的噪声特性与个体差异，明确了从连续坐标到离散输入事件的关键约束。其次，设计并实现了归一化、个体标定与时序平滑组成的实时优化链路，并在此基础上构建命中判定、停留触发、文本状态管理与会话记录模块，形成端到端眼控输入闭环。最后，在统一口径下完成基础链路、固定文本任务、重复实验和失败案例验证。结果表明，系统在6轮端到端任务中达到exact_match_rate=1.0、mean_CER=0.0，且在效率与稳定性指标上保持一致性，验证了所提方法对低成本无障碍眼控输入的有效性。

关键词 眼控输入;  无障碍交互;  视线估计;  OpenFace;  停留触发

【英文题目】
Real-Time Gaze Estimation Optimization Based on OpenFace

【英文摘要】
Abstract This thesis targets accessibility-oriented interaction and addresses three practical issues in webcam-based eye-controlled input: gaze jitter, spatial bias, and false triggering. The work first analyzes the noise characteristics and user-specific variability of OpenFace gaze observations in real-time use, and then defines the key constraints for converting continuous coordinates into discrete input events. A layered optimization pipeline is designed and implemented, including normalization, personal calibration, and temporal smoothing. On top of this pipeline, a complete interaction loop is built with hit testing, dwell triggering, text state management, and session logging. Experiments are conducted under a unified protocol, covering baseline signal quality, fixed-text end-to-end tasks, repeated-run stability, and failure-case diagnosis. Results show an exact match rate of 1.0 and a mean CER of 0.0 over 6 end-to-end runs, with stable efficiency metrics, indicating that the proposed method is effective for low-cost accessibility-focused eye-controlled input.

Keywords eye-controlled input;  accessibility interaction;  gaze estimation;  OpenFace;  dwell triggering
